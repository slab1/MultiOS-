## Branding Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size**: 0.5" wide for print
- **Clear Space**: Minimum 0.25" around logo
- **Color Variations**: 
  - Full color (preferred)
  - Single color (white/black)
  - Reverse (white on dark)
- **Placement**: Left chest for shirts, centered for hoodies

### Color Specifications
- **Primary Blue**: #2C3E50 (CMYK: 70, 55, 20, 5)
- **Secondary Purple**: #8E44AD (CMYK: 35, 75, 0, 25)
- **Accent Green**: #27AE60 (CMYK: 65, 0, 85, 0)
- **Text Colors**: 
  - White on dark backgrounds
  - Black on light backgrounds
  - Navy on white backgrounds

### Typography Guidelines
- **Headlines**: Helvetica Neue Bold
- **Body Text**: Helvetica Neue Regular
- **Code Snippets**: Monaco or Courier New
- **Hierarchy**: Consistent sizing and spacing
