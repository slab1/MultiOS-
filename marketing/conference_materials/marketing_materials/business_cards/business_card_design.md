## Technical Specifications

### Print Specifications
- **Size**: 3.5" x 2" (standard business card)
- **Bleed**: 0.125" on all sides
- **Safe Area**: 0.25" inside trim
- **Paper**: 100lb cover weight, premium cardstock
- **Finish Options**:
  - Matte with spot UV on logo
  - Satin with foil stamping on logo
  - Linen texture for premium feel

### Color Specifications
- **Primary Colors**:
  - MultiOS Blue: #2C3E50 (CMYK: 70, 55, 20, 5)
  - MultiOS Purple: #8E44AD (CMYK: 35, 75, 0, 25)
  - MultiOS Green: #27AE60 (CMYK: 65, 0, 85, 0)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Helvetica Neue (Bold for logo, Regular for text)
- **Alternative**: Open Sans (for web compatibility)
- **Logo Font**: Custom MultiOS font (if available)

### Logo Specifications
- **Minimum Size**: 0.75" wide
- **Clear Space**: 0.25" around logo
- **File Formats**: AI, EPS, PDF, PNG (300 DPI)
- **Color Variations**: Full color, grayscale, single color

### QR Code Specifications
- **Size**: 0.75" x 0.75" (minimum for print)
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI
- **Error Correction**: Level M (15% for print)
- **Contrast**: High contrast black on white

## Usage Guidelines

### Distribution Strategy
1. **Conference Booth**: Primary distribution point
2. **Academic Conferences**: Faculty and researcher focus
3. **Student Events**: Ambassador and student focus
4. **Industry Events**: Partnership and recruitment focus
5. **Online**: Digital distribution for remote networking

### Professional Use
1. **Networking Events**: Exchange during introductions
2. **Follow-up Communications**: Include in emails
3. **Digital Signatures**: Add to email signatures
4. **Website**: Include on team pages
5. **Social Media**: Share in LinkedIn profiles

### Design Consistency
1. **Maintain Brand Colors**: Always use approved color palette
2. **Logo Placement**: Follow established positioning rules
3. **Font Usage**: Stick to approved typography
4. **Quality**: Only use high-resolution, print-ready files
5. **Updates**: Refresh designs annually or with major updates

### Special Versions
1. **QR Code Cards**: For tech-savvy audiences
2. **NFC Cards**: For advanced networking (if budget allows)
3. **Die-cut Cards**: Unique shapes for special events
4. **Recycled Cards**: For sustainability-focused events
5. **Large Format**: For booth displays and banners

## Printing and Production

### Recommended Printers
- **Local Print Shop**: For quick turnaround and small quantities
- **Online Printers**: For bulk orders and specialty finishes
  - Moo.com (premium quality)
  - PrintRunner (cost-effective)
  - PrintPlace (variety of options)

### Cost Estimates (per 500 cards)
- **Basic Cards**: $50-75 (matte finish)
- **Premium Cards**: $100-150 (spot UV, foil)
- **Specialty Cards**: $200-300 (die-cut, unique materials)
- **Digital Cards**: $0-10 (vCard, online distribution)

### Production Timeline
- **Design Finalization**: 3-5 days
- **Proof Approval**: 1-2 days
- **Printing**: 5-7 days (standard), 2-3 days (rush)
- **Delivery**: 2-5 days
- **Total**: 11-19 days (standard)

### Quality Control
1. **Color Matching**: Ensure CMYK accuracy
2. **Font Rendering**: Verify all fonts display correctly
3. **Logo Quality**: Check logo reproduction at card size
4. **QR Code Testing**: Scan test codes before printing
5. **Paper Quality**: Inspect cardstock feel and durability
