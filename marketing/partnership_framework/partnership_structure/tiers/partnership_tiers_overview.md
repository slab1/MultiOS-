# MultiOS Partnership Tiers Overview

## Tier Structure

MultiOS offers four partnership tiers designed to accommodate institutions of different sizes, engagement levels, and educational objectives.

### Bronze Partnership (Entry Level)
**Target Institutions:** Small colleges, community centers, coding bootcamps
**Student Capacity:** 1-500 students

#### Benefits:
- **Educational License:** Up to 500 student licenses
- **Basic Support:** Email support, 24-48 hour response time
- **Training:** Access to online certification courses (up to 5 faculty members)
- **Resources:** Standard curriculum materials and documentation
- **Community:** Access to MultiOS educational community forum
- **Updates:** Quarterly software updates and patches

#### Requirements:
- Annual partnership fee: $2,500
- Commitment to teach at least one MultiOS course per semester
- Provide annual usage reports
- Designate a faculty liaison for communication

#### Support Level:
- Email support
- Online documentation and tutorials
- Community forum access
- Quarterly check-in calls

---

### Silver Partnership (Standard Level)
**Target Institutions:** Mid-size universities, specialized technical schools
**Student Capacity:** 501-2,000 students

#### Benefits:
- **Educational License:** Up to 2,000 student licenses
- **Enhanced Support:** Priority email support, 12-24 hour response time
- **Training:** Comprehensive faculty training for up to 15 instructors
- **Resources:** Advanced curriculum materials, lab exercises, and project templates
- **Customization:** Minor curriculum customization support
- **Updates:** Bi-monthly software updates and feature releases
- **Events:** Participation in MultiOS educational conferences

#### Requirements:
- Annual partnership fee: $7,500
- Offer at least two MultiOS-related courses or programs
- Provide semester usage reports with student feedback
- Designate dedicated faculty coordinator
- Participate in annual partner assessment

#### Support Level:
- Priority email and phone support
- Dedicated success manager
- Monthly strategy calls
- Access to beta features and testing programs

---

### Gold Partnership (Advanced Level)
**Target Institutions:** Large universities, research institutions
**Student Capacity:** 2,001-10,000 students

#### Benefits:
- **Educational License:** Up to 10,000 student licenses
- **Premium Support:** Phone and email support, 4-8 hour response time
- **Training:** Extensive faculty training program for unlimited instructors
- **Resources:** Full curriculum development support, custom lab environments
- **Research:** Access to research collaboration opportunities
- **Events:** Speaking opportunities at conferences, sponsored events
- **Updates:** Monthly updates with early access to new features
- **Consulting:** Implementation consulting and best practices guidance

#### Requirements:
- Annual partnership fee: $25,000
- Offer comprehensive MultiOS curriculum (minimum 3 courses/programs)
- Provide quarterly detailed reports including research outcomes
- Designate full-time partnership coordinator
- Commit to joint research or development projects

#### Support Level:
- Dedicated technical account manager
- Priority phone support with SLA guarantees
- Bi-weekly strategy and planning calls
- Custom feature requests consideration

---

### Platinum Partnership (Elite Level)
**Target Institutions:** Flagship universities, major research institutions
**Student Capacity:** Unlimited

#### Benefits:
- **Educational License:** Unlimited student and faculty licenses
- **White-Glove Support:** Dedicated support team, 1-2 hour response time
- **Training:** Executive training programs, unlimited faculty certification
- **Resources:** Full curriculum co-development, custom platform features
- **Research:** Joint research initiatives, publication support, grant assistance
- **Events:** Co-branded events, premier conference speaking opportunities
- **Updates:** Priority access to all updates and new platform capabilities
- **Innovation:** Access to MultiOS innovation lab and beta programs
- **Strategic Planning:** Annual strategic planning sessions

#### Requirements:
- Annual partnership fee: $50,000+ (based on institution size)
- Offer comprehensive MultiOS degree programs or tracks
- Provide monthly detailed analytics and research reports
- Designate executive-level partnership champion
- Commit to multi-year strategic partnership agreement
- Participate in MultiOS advisory board

#### Support Level:
- Executive relationship manager
- 24/7 priority support with SLA guarantees
- Weekly executive briefings
- Custom development consideration for major requests

## Tier Upgrade Path

### Automatic Upgrades
- Institutions can request tier upgrades at any time
- Pro-rated pricing for mid-year upgrades
- All benefits of new tier effective immediately upon approval

### Performance-Based Incentives
- **Bronze to Silver:** Complimentary upgrade for institutions teaching 3+ courses annually
- **Silver to Gold:** 25% discount on upgrade fee for institutions with 95%+ satisfaction ratings
- **Gold to Platinum:** Complimentary upgrade for institutions contributing to core platform development

### Long-term Commitment Benefits
- **3-year agreements:** 15% discount on annual fees
- **5-year agreements:** 25% discount on annual fees
- **Multi-institution networks:** Special pricing for coordinated rollouts across multiple campuses

## Special Partnership Categories

### Research Partner (Add-on to any tier)
- Additional $10,000 annual fee
- Access to research version of MultiOS
- Technical support for research projects
- Publication and citation guidelines
- Conference presentation opportunities

### Developer Partner (For institutions with strong CS programs)
- Additional $5,000 annual fee
- Access to MultiOS source code for educational purposes
- Bug reporting and feature request privileges
- Student internship and job placement pipeline
- Faculty sabbatical and visiting researcher opportunities

### Community College Network Partner
- Special pricing for networks of 5+ community colleges
- Shared resources and best practices
- Coordinated faculty training programs
- Standardized curriculum across network
- Joint grant application support

---

## Geographic and Institutional Special Considerations

### International Partnerships
- Currency-based pricing with special regional rates
- Cultural adaptation support for curriculum materials
- Multi-language documentation and support
- Regional compliance and data privacy considerations

### Small/Rural Institution Support
- Reduced pricing based on student population and geographic factors
- Virtual training and support options
- Travel support for key personnel to attend training events
- Extended implementation timelines

### Historical Black Colleges and Universities (HBCU) Program
- Special partnership terms and pricing
- Dedicated support for unique institutional needs
- Scholarship programs for student developers
- Community outreach and engagement opportunities
