# Partnership Application and Approval Process

## Application Process Overview

The MultiOS partnership application process is designed to be transparent, efficient, and aligned with institutional goals. The entire process typically takes 4-6 weeks from initial inquiry to signed agreement.

## Step 1: Initial Inquiry and Interest Assessment

### Online Interest Form
**Duration:** 5-10 minutes

**Information Required:**
- Institution name and type (university, college, technical school, etc.)
- Location and student population
- Current operating systems used in computer science education
- Existing curriculum offerings
- Contact information for partnership discussions

**Submit:** [Online Interest Form](https://multios.edu/partners/interest)

**Response Timeline:** 24-48 hours

### Initial Consultation Call
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Participants:** Institution representatives + MultiOS Partnership Team

**Agenda:**
- Discuss institutional goals and MultiOS alignment
- Review preliminary requirements and benefits
- Assess technical infrastructure requirements
- Answer questions about MultiOS platform
- Provide preliminary cost estimate based on tier selection

## Step 2: Formal Partnership Application

### Required Documentation

#### Institution Profile
- [ ] Institution accreditation status and documentation
- [ ] Computer Science curriculum overview
- [ ] Current technology infrastructure assessment
- [ ] Faculty qualifications and expertise areas

## Step 3: Application Review and Evaluation

### Initial Review (3-5 business days)
**Review Team:** Partnership Manager + Technical Assessment Team

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- **Institutional Readiness:** Accreditation, faculty expertise, technical infrastructure
- **Educational Alignment:** Curriculum integration potential, learning outcomes alignment
- **Commitment Level:** Proposed course offerings, faculty involvement, student reach
- **Technical Viability:** Infrastructure compatibility, support capabilities
- **Strategic Fit:** Partnership goals alignment, long-term potential

### Technical Assessment (5-7 business days)
**Assessment Team:** Senior Technical Architects + Implementation Specialists

**Technical Evaluation:**
- Hardware compatibility and requirements
- Network infrastructure and security protocols
- Data management and compliance capabilities
- Technical support team assessment
- Integration with existing systems (LMS, authentication, etc.)

### Educational Review (3-5 business days)
**Review Team:** Educational Content Directors + Academic Advisory Board

**Educational Evaluation:**
- Curriculum design and learning objectives
- Faculty development and training plans
- Student assessment and outcome measurement
- Research and innovation opportunities
- Community and industry engagement potential

---

## Step 4: Partnership Recommendation and Negotiation

### Partnership Proposal Development
**Duration:** 3-5 business days after review completion

**Proposal Components:**
- Recommended partnership tier
- Customized benefit package
- Implementation timeline
- Training and support plan
- Pricing and payment structure
- Success metrics and evaluation criteria

### Negotiation and Customization
**Duration:** 5-10 business days
**Participants:** Institution leadership + MultiOS Executive Team

**Negotiation Topics:**
- Tier selection and benefit customization
- Implementation timeline and milestones
- Training schedule and resource allocation
- Support level adjustments
- Pricing and payment terms
- Success metrics and reporting requirements

---

## Step 5: Agreement Preparation and Execution

### Contract Development
**Duration:** 5-7 business days
**Participants:** Legal teams from both organizations

**Contract Components:**
- Partnership terms and conditions
- Service level agreements (SLAs)
- Intellectual property and licensing terms
- Data privacy and security requirements
- Financial terms and payment schedules
- Termination and renewal procedures
- Performance metrics and reporting requirements

### Legal Review and Approval
**Duration:** 5-10 business days
**Process:** Parallel review by both institution and MultiOS legal teams

**Review Focus:**
- Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Intellectual property protection
- Data privacy and security compliance
- Financial terms and payment security

### Contract Execution
**Duration:** 1-2 business days
**Process:** Electronic signature execution by authorized representatives

**Required Signatures:**
- Institution: President/Chancellor or authorized designate
- MultiOS: CEO or authorized partnership officer

## Common Application Questions and Answers

### Q: What if our institution doesn't meet all technical requirements?
**A:** We provide technical consultation and can work with your IT team to develop a remediation plan. Minor deficiencies can be addressed during the implementation phase.

### Q: Can we start with a lower tier and upgrade later?
**A:** Absolutely! Many institutions begin with Bronze or Silver and upgrade as their program grows. We provide upgrade paths with pro-rated pricing.

### Q: What support do you provide during the application process?
**A:** You'll have a dedicated partnership manager who can answer questions, provide guidance, and help ensure your application is complete and competitive.

### Q: How long does the partnership agreement last?
**A:** Standard agreements are 3 years, with options for 1-year pilot programs or 5-year strategic partnerships. Renewal discussions begin 6 months before expiration.

### Q: What happens if we need to modify the agreement?
**A:** We understand that institutional needs change. Mid-term modifications can be negotiated, with changes typically taking effect at the beginning of the next academic term.
