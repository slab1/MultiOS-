# MultiOS Educational Partnership Framework

## Overview

This comprehensive partnership framework provides universities and schools with a complete roadmap for adopting and implementing MultiOS in their computer science education programs. The framework includes detailed documentation, templates, tools, and resources to ensure successful partnership and implementation.

## Framework Components

### 1. Partnership Program Structure (`partnership_structure/`)

**Tiered Partnership Levels** (`tiers/`)
- Bronze Partnership (Entry Level): $2,500/year, 1-500 students
- Silver Partnership (Standard Level): $7,500/year, 501-2,000 students  
- Gold Partnership (Advanced Level): $25,000/year, 2,001-10,000 students
- Platinum Partnership (Elite Level): $50,000+/year, unlimited students

**Application Process** (`processes/`)
- 4-6 week comprehensive application process
- Transparent evaluation criteria and requirements
- Fast-track options for qualified institutions
- Online status tracking and communication portal

**Contract Templates** (`contracts/`)
- Comprehensive partnership agreement templates
- Customizable terms and service level agreements
- Intellectual property and licensing frameworks
- Performance metrics and evaluation criteria

**Revenue Sharing Models** (`licensing/`)
- Flexible licensing options (Institutional, Course-based, Department, Network)
- Revenue sharing opportunities based on student success
- Special pricing programs for startups and international institutions
- Multi-year commitment incentives
