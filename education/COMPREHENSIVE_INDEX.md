## Overview

Welcome to the comprehensive educational resource center for **MultiOS** - the universal educational operating system written in Rust. Our mission is to provide world-class learning experiences that take you from complete beginner to expert-level operating systems developer.

### What is MultiOS?

MultiOS is a **universal educational operating system** designed specifically for learning and research. It supports multiple architectures (x86_64, ARM64, RISC-V) and provides a modern, safe development environment for exploring operating systems concepts.

### Why Learn with MultiOS?

- üåç **Multi-Architecture Support**: Learn once, deploy everywhere
- ü¶Ä **Rust-Powered**: Modern, safe systems programming language
- üéØ **Education-First**: Purpose-built for learning and teaching
- ü§ù **Community-Driven**: Open source, collaborative learning
- üèÜ **Industry-Relevant**: Skills that matter in professional environments
